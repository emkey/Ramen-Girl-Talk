The Ramen Girl

Each bowl of ramen you prepare is a gift to your customer. The food that you serve your customer becomes a part of them. It contains your spirit. That's why your ramen must be an expression of pure love. A gift from your heart.

Our Products become a part of our users lives


A bowl of ramen is a self-contained universe, with life from the sea, the mountains, and the earth. All existing in perfect harmony. Harmony is essential. What holds it all together is the broth. The broth gives life to the ramen.

Design Thinking Excercise

How might we help college students make better spending decisions?

Plan and Design a web/mobile app that will help you keep better track of your expenses.

  1. Features
  2. Aesthetics
  3. Wireframe/Prototype
Start by asking yourself about your problems with money, banking, allowance and spending and prepare a list of questions. Think about your spending habits and how problems with money
Approach one group, and ask them about their spending habits, their problems with money. Try as much as possible to be on the passive side. Go for the who what why? how? how does it feel? What do you feel with each item you spend? Do the same with another group.
